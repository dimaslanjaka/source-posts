#### Anonymous proxy:

no HTTP\_X\_FORWARDED\_FOR header  

#### High anonymous/Elite proxy:

no HTTP\_X\_FORWARDED\_FOR, HTTP\_VIA, HTTP\_FORWARDED, HTTP\_X\_CLUSTER\_CLIENT\_IP, HTTP\_CLIENT\_IP, HTTP\_PROXY\_CONNECTION headers
